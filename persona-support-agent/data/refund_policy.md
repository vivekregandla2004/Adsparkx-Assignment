# Refund Policy

## Overview

We want you to be completely satisfied with your subscription. This document outlines when refunds are available, how to request them, and what to expect during processing.

---

## Refund Eligibility

### 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee (New Customers)

If you are a **new customer** on your **first subscription**, you are eligible for a full refund within **30 days** of your initial purchase — no questions asked.

**Conditions:**
- Applies to the first payment only
- Does not apply to add-ons or premium features purchased separately
- Account must not have violated Terms of Service
- Request must be submitted within 30 calendar days of the first charge

### Pro-Rated Refunds (Annual Plans)

If you cancel an annual plan **before the 6-month mark**, you may be eligible for a prorated refund for unused months.

| Cancellation Timing | Refund Eligibility |
|--------------------|--------------------|
| Within 30 days | Full refund (new customers) or pro-rated |
| 31–180 days (6 months) | Pro-rated refund for remaining months |
| After 180 days | No refund; access continues until year end |

**Pro-rated refund formula:**
```
Refund = (Annual Price / 12) × Remaining Full Months
```

### Non-Refundable Items

The following are **not eligible for refunds** under any circumstances:
- Monthly subscription fees (current period)
- Setup fees or onboarding fees
- Professional services or consulting hours
- Overage charges (API calls, storage)
- Custom development work
- Add-ons consumed in the current billing period

## How to Request a Refund

### Step 1: Submit a Refund Request

1. Log into your account at `https://platform.example.com`
2. Go to **Billing → Refund Request**
3. Select the invoice or charge you wish to dispute
4. Choose the refund reason:
   - Technical issues preventing use
   - Service did not meet expectations
   - Accidental purchase
   - Business closure
   - Other (please specify)
5. Provide additional details in the text field (optional but helpful)
6. Submit the form

Alternatively, email refunds@example.com with subject: **REFUND REQUEST - [Your Account Email]**

### Step 2: Review Period

Our billing team reviews all refund requests within **3–5 business days**.

For expedited review (eligible for Enterprise customers):
- Email refunds@example.com with "URGENT" in the subject line
- Expected review: within 1 business day

### Step 3: Approval and Processing

- **Approved refunds** are processed within **7–10 business days**
- Refund is returned to the original payment method
- Credit card refunds may take an additional 3–5 business days to appear, depending on your bank
- PayPal refunds typically appear within 24 hours
- Bank transfer refunds (ACH) take 3–5 business days

---

## Refund Dispute Process

If your refund request is denied and you believe it was incorrectly assessed:

1. Reply to the denial email with additional supporting evidence
2. Or contact billing@example.com and reference your ticket number
3. A senior billing specialist will review within 2 business days

If you believe there was a billing error (wrong amount charged, duplicate charge), see the Billing Policy for the dispute process.

---

## Special Circumstances

### Service Outages

If an outage lasting more than **4 consecutive hours** occurs and affects your service, you are entitled to a **service credit** equal to 3× the downtime as a percentage of your monthly fee.

Example: 4-hour outage in a 30-day month = 0.56% of monthly fee × 3 = 1.67% credit

Service credits are applied automatically to your next invoice. They are not cash refunds.

### Force Majeure

Refunds are not guaranteed in cases of service disruption caused by events beyond our control (natural disasters, government actions, internet infrastructure failures).
